Rainbow Cupcakes

Ingredients
  • 225g Butter
  • 475g sugar
  • 5 egg whites
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla essence
  • 375g plain flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 125ml milk, warmed to room temperature
  • Gel food colouring in rainbow colours: Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Purple
Method
  1. Preheat oven to 175C
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together until smooth and light in colour. Then add in the egg whites and vanilla.
  3. Mix the flour, salt and baking powder in a separate bowl.
  4. Add the dry ingredients and milk to the creamed mixture.
  5. Separate the cake mix into six separate bowls and colour each bowl with a separate colouring, mixing until the colours are fully combined
  6. Add a thin layer of each mixture to cupcake cases, beginning with purple and working back to red.
  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes.
Icing Ingredients Method
  • 225g softened butter
  • 500g icing sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla essence
  • 3 tablespoons of milk
  • A pinch of salt

To make the icing:Whip the butter until light in colour. Add the vanilla essence. Then slowly add the sugar and milk, alternating between the two. Mix until light and fluffy.

To ice the cupcakes:Use a piping bag with a closed star tip to pipe the icing onto each cupcake.
