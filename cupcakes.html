Cupcake Recipes

Ingredients
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 175g Plain flour
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 195g finely shredded carrots
  • 140g Granulated Sugar
  • 80g Light Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 65g unsweetened applesauce
  • 120ml Vegetable Oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
Method
  1. Preheat the oven to 175 C
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg for 30 seconds, set aside. Shred carrots as directed in notes below, set aside.
  3. In a separate large mixing bowl, whisk together granulated sugar and brown sugar, then add eggs and using an electric hand mixer, blend mixture on low speed 30 seconds, until slightly pale.
  4. Mix in applesauce and vanilla extract. With mixer running on low speed, slowly pour in vegetable oil and mix until combined, then mix 20 seconds longer
  5. Mix in half of the dry ingredients, then pour the shredded carrots into the bowl with the remaining flour mixture and toss in flour mixture to coat carrots (they'll clump together a bit but that's fine)
  6. Pour carrot mixture into the batter and blend until evenly combined.
  7. Pour the batter into paper-lined muffin cups, filling each cup about 3/4 full. Bake in preheated oven about 19 - 21 minutes.

Icing Ingredients Method
  • 110g softened, unsalted butter
  • 225g cream cheese
  • 410g Icing Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
To make the icing: Using an electric mixer whip together butter and cream cheese on medium-high speed until smooth and fluffy. Mix in vanilla extract. With mixer running on low speed, slowly add powdered sugar and beat until well blended and smooth. If frosting is slightly runny freeze several minutes then mix again and repeat if needed.
